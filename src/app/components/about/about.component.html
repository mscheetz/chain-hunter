About

Multi-Blockchain Explorer

After repeating the arduous task of checking multiple explorer websites to track down address or transaction details, the brain-child behind The Chain Hunter decided enough was enough and The Chain Hunter was created! The Chain Hunter was built to ease this effort and make a one-stop shop for you all your blockchain querying needs.

The Chain Hunter now supports a large array of blockchain projects and offers the ability to save searches of addresses, contracts, and or transactions.
